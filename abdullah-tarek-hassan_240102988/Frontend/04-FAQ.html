Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about VitalTrack and your health journey.

What is BMI? +
BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight to measure body fatness. It applies to most adult men and women aged 20 and over.
Is VitalTrack free to use? +
Yes! VitalTrack is currently free for all users. You can track your BMI and monitor your progress without any cost.
How is my data stored? +
Your data is stored securely in your browser's local storage for the demo version. In the full version, we use a secure database to keep your history safe.
Can I use VitalTrack on mobile? +
Absolutely! Our website is fully responsive and works great on smartphones and tablets.
